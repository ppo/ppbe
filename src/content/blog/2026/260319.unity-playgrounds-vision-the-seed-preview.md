## The Seed

Do you feel it? That quiet sense that something is fundamentally off. Not just one thing – everything. And that no election, no policy, no technology update is going to fix it. Does our modern society feels healthy? And sustainable? Do you feel the unhappiness and disconnection of the young generations?

Humanity evolved naturally over millennia – scattered developments that eventually merged into today’s global patchwork. Primitive experiments that were never designed to work together, created by separated peoples without any overarching vision.

Everything started from a few people living in a scarce environment who joined forces into a tribe to survive. This pattern emerged naturally all over the world because it’s deeply rooted into human’s nature – we instinctively know that Unity Makes Strength.

Each tribe developed its own rules, behaviors, and customs. Some tribes managed to merge with different levels of success. Some didn’t trust the other tribes or were fundamentally incompatible.

Living in scarcity, survival instincts made people protect their possessions, and therefore develop a separation mindset – “me against others”. That mindset made people exchange goods rather than sharing – which deepened the division. And also led some people to stealing and fighting each other over resources – out of necessity or laziness.

Times have changed. We have evolved. We have accumulated incredible knowledge, developed amazing technologies, and are now truly global – and that changes everything!

Take a step back to reflect and think. Can we continue to apply patches and fixes here and there? Are our current disparate foundations able to sustain our evolution?

Nowadays, we are capable of creating true abundance for everyone. Well… if we dare to revive our true nature, the one that instinctively united people into tribes, the one that knows that Unity Makes Strength.

Now that we’re truly global, what was initially a tribe is now humanity itself – the whole human race! Now we could join forces not to survive but to thrive.

The time has come to consciously decide to shift into a new paradigm, to start the Unity Era.

Isn’t it the right time to ask ourselves:

<p class="indent">
    <strong>If we wanted to start all over from scratch, given the current state of our knowledge and technologies, what would an intelligent civilization do?</strong>
</p>

Are we finally going to decide to become an intelligent civilization?  
Starting with you – are _you_ in?

No? It’s OK, this isn’t for everyone. At this stage, we need dreamers, pioneers, and early adopters. We’re looking for people who dare to explore the unknown, to take a leap of faith. Continue reading though, you might change your mind 😉
